## Major Theories

### Theory: The World is a Dream

**Confidence:** Speculative

Evidence suggests the Kingdom Death world may be:
- A dream of a cosmic entity
- A pocket dimension created for unknown purposes
- A testing ground for humanity

**Supporting Evidence:**
- The Ethereal Dreamer's existence
- Reality-bending phenomena
- Cyclical nature of campaigns

### Theory: Monsters Are Transformed Humans

**Confidence:** Likely

Some monsters may have once been human:
- The Butcher's human-like behavior
- Nemesis monsters that "hunt" survivors specifically
- The King's humanoid appearance

### Theory: The Entity Farms Humanity

**Confidence:** Speculative

The Entity may cultivate human settlements like crops:
- Survivors as resources
- Campaigns as harvest cycles
- Evolution toward specific outcomes

### Theory: Multiple Realities Overlap

**Confidence:** Likely

Evidence for multiple timelines/dimensions:
- The Lantern Festival
- Time paradoxes in story events
- Characters appearing across eras

## How to Evaluate Theories

| Confidence | Meaning |
|------------|---------|
| **Confirmed** | Stated in official materials |
| **Likely** | Strong circumstantial evidence |
| **Speculative** | Interesting but unproven |
| **Debunked** | Contradicted by canon |

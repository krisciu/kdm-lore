# The World of Kingdom Death

The Kingdom Death universe is a realm of eternal darkness, nightmarish creatures, and struggling human settlements. This section explores the fundamental nature of this twisted reality.

## Overview

The world of Kingdom Death exists in perpetual darkness, illuminated only by the soft glow of lanterns that humanity has learned to craft. There is no sun, no stars—only an endless expanse of stone faces and lurking horrors.

## Key Themes

1. **Eternal Darkness** - No natural light exists
2. **Hostile Environment** - Everything wants to kill survivors
3. **Mysterious Origin** - How did humanity come to be here?
4. **Cycles of Existence** - Time works differently in this realm

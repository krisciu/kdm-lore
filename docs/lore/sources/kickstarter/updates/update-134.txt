## KINGDOM DEATH PHOBIA COMIC PART 2 AND BEYOND!

Fucking amazing news! We sold enough comics to cover the production of the next 3 parts! Pawel has shared with me, his ambition for his story spans 6 comics in total! 

Oh! And an interesting note about the development of these. I AM NOT INVOLVED! We decided that his viewpoint as a fan and his interpretation of the Kingdom Death world, was paramount. Pawel will ask me very specific world rule or lore based questions, which I take back to the team so we can carefully answer them. Giving these comics both a unique viewpoint and an authenticity that I think is hard to match.

## MADNESS!!!

[IMAGE]

In 2025, we added a few new illustrator / animators to the team. Why? You might be asking yourself? Well... we do no marketing! And while the core game was being reprinted, Anna and I had several sincere discussions about our little brand and our love for it. We started to wonder, how we make an earnest effort to reach out to new potential fans? We have been focused on just you guys, our core supports, fans, backers and bakcers since 2017! 

So I asked around - everyone's professional advice was the same. "Spend money on keywords". And I dunno, at that time I felt a little repulsed by the idea. Not to say I am so vain that I think everything I do is right, its quite the opposite! I just put all my focus into quality and creation, I am creator after all, not a marketing expert! 

SOOOO we thought, maybe we could spend this little marketing budget on something more meaningful for everyone than you know, keywords (not to say visibility or marketing is bad!) TURNS OUT MAKING ANIMATIONS IS REALLY HARD AND TIME CONSUMING.... A PERFECT FIT FOR KINGDOM DEATH! BWA HAHAHAHAHAHA.

We don't have a studio or anything crazy like that, just a tiny collection of freelancers that were blown away by the brand and wanted to lend a hand. 

My goal, to put us in a position where we have as much fun as possible, with a little sprinkle of emotes and animations here and there to celebrate our love for KD AS WE RACE TOWARDS OUR KICKSTARTER FINISH LINE! I am very serious about having this all wrapped by 2027. THATS TEN YEARS IT HAS TO BE DONE! 

AND I WANNA PARTY WHEN WE GET THERE!

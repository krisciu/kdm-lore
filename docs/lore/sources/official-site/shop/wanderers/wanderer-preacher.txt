## Product Description

**This is a preorder. It will be available to ship Q1 2026.**

Wanderers are new to Kingdom Death: Monster with the release of the Gambler's Chest expansion. Any campaign can now add a wanderer, a mysterious stranger that makes your settlement their temporary home.

As they fight alongside your settlement, their own story unfolds. Secrets, skills, and more are shared by your visitor. Cultivate a relationship as the wanderer fights along with your survivors to protect your settlement.

**She arrives with benevolence. When the settlement falls under her spell, strange dogma takes root.**

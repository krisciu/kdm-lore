## Lore / Description

+4 population! A brand new crop of survivor miniatures perfect for your Monster game! Featuring four sculpts of strangers struggling to survive the darkness. This set includes a range of rules and tools representing the ways survivors might harness the world around them for their own gain. From indomitable resources to carve from the toughest monsters, to seed patterns of inspired design, to the knowledges of the arc survivors, this box celebrates all the advantages to be found in this meager existence.
Add their rules to your campaign or use these dynamic models to represent your favorite Monster survivors.
Art & Sculpture: Team Death
The Survivors of Death II custom box includes:
1 x Crabman plastic miniature
1 x Valkyrie plastic miniature
1 x Dark Paladin plastic miniature
1 x Priest plastic miniature
4 x Hard plastic 30mm insert
4 x Hard plastic 30mm bases
4 x Large matte print of the original concept artwork on heavy card stock
4 x Character cards: The Crabby, The Disinterested, The Ardent, Sanctimonious
This product is best experienced with Gambler's Chest and Frogdog Expansions.
Priest is accompanied by:
1 x Umbilical Phalanges Tyrant Indomitable resource card
1 x Inquisitorial Saw pattern card
1 x Inquisitorial Sawp attern gear card
For best experience use with People of the Stars from the Dragon King expansion
Dark Paladin is accompanied by:
1 x Perceptual Isolation collective cognition card (replaces other CC 19 card in your collective cognition deck
3.x Knowledge cards (Placid Mentality I, Placid Mentality II, Placid Mentality III)
Valkyrie is accompanied by:
1 x Fast Twitch Strain strain milestone card
1 x Soaring Advance strain fighting art
Crabman is accompanied by:
2 x Seed pattern card: Sinistral Crabaxe and Dextral Crabaxe

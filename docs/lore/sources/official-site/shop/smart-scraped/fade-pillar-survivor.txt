Fade violated the White Speakers' oath against childbearing. Expecting no mercy, she fled the cult with her child and the Sword of Silence stolen from the Sword Hunters' armory.
Pillar cards are used during the design of your new campaign. They add rules that increase the depth of your campaign experience. Campaigns of Death Vol 1 (CoD) includes complete rules for campaign design that includes pillar cards.
Using Fade pillar card in your next campaign
When you design your next campaign, if you want to include this pillar, add the listed campaign milestone (When the settlement innovates Storytelling | Replace a basic hunt event with Baby and the Sword) with other Milestone Story Events on your settlement record sheet.

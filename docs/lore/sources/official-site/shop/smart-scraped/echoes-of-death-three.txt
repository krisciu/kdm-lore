## Lore / Description

Echoes of Death 3 includes 4 new survivors that embody memories from another world. These memories can be unlocked by reaching unique milestones in the story of your campaign.
This set includes prototypes of Strain System mechanics. Add your Strain Milestone cards to your game, if you reach their unique conditions, your gameplay changes forever. A new fighting art borne from distant dreams is permanently added to your game.
Art: Lokman LamSculpture: Team Death
The Echoes of Death 3 custom box includes:
1 x Knight Survivor with scenic base insert
1 x Sniper Survivor  with scenic base insert
1 x Monk Survivor with scenic base insert
1 x Dark Paladin Survivor
1 x Hard plastic 30mm insert
4 x Hard plastic 30mm bases
4 x Large matte print of the original concept artwork on heavy card stock
1 x prototype Strain Card rules sheet for adding to your campaign
4 x Strain Milestone cards
4 x Strain Fighting Arts
1 x Vermin Card
1 x Strain Fighting Arts card divider
1 x Strain Milestone card divider

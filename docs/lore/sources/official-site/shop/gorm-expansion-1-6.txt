## Gorm Lore

Gorm travel enormous distances in their life cycles, shaping the landscapes with their enormous appetites and extreme bodily functions. Young Gorm roam the darkness devouring anything that moves (and many things that don't). During their mating season, older Gorm produces storms of incredible destruction. Ancient Gorm that have lived hundreds of lantern years will make a final solitary march to the fabled gormyards to die. The Gorm is a volatile quarry, but the complexities of its anatomy can unlock powerful tools for the settlement.

## Game Information

The Gorm [NQ1] is a node 1 quarry monster, it provides early settlements with their first sustenance and resources. This expansion replaces the White Lion in Kingdom Death: Monster campaigns.

## Node System Explanation

Kingdom Death: Monster is played as a campaign stretching over many lantern years (and game sessions) to tell the story of your settlement.

Countless varieties of Monster campaigns are easy to create using a node system. All monsters in a campaign have a node. Nodes represent monsters' difficulty. The monsters of the core game are:

- [NQ1] White Lion
- [NQ2] Screaming Antelope
- [NQ3] Phoenix
- [NN1] Butcher
- [NN2] King's Man
- [NN3] Hand
- [Core] Watcher
- [Finale] Gold Smoke Knight

When you purchase a Monster expansion, replace a core game monster of the same node with your new expansion monster.

## Lore Notes

### Gorm Life Cycle
1. Young Gorm - Roam and devour everything
2. Adult Gorm - Produce destructive storms during mating season
3. Ancient Gorm - March to gormyards to die

### Key Characteristics
- Travels enormous distances
- Shapes landscapes through appetite and bodily functions
- Volatile nature
- Complex anatomy useful for crafting

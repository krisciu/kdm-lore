## Frogdog Lore

Barks and croaks in the darkness coalesce into a plaintive song. A putrid foe lurks at the start of your campaign.

Cagey and revolting the monster leaps into action. The monster's unique physiology creates wobbling bubbles of stench that pursue the bioelectric hum of survivors as they take to the air in pursuit of the incomprehensible behemoth.

### Waterlogged Beast - Leaps Into Action

The Frogdog lurks in overgrown pools. Its indiscriminate appetite creates turbulent accumulation of gases in its distended gut. When threatened, the monster weaponizes its gas to ruin its enemies then leap into the darkness powered by its well muscled legs.

This monster is the first monster awaiting the newly awoken survivors in their journey.

## Game Information

The Frogdog is a new cornerstone quarry monster for Kingdom Death: Monster.

The Frogdog is a node 1 quarry monster and takes the place of the White Lion in your core game campaign.

Victory provides a burgeoning settlement with a surprising new set of tools for survival.

## Key Features

### Prologuedog
This expansion includes a new prologue version of the monster. The Frogdog devours the White Lion, taking its place in opening story of your campaign.

**Devour the White Lion** story event presents a Frogdog variant to replace the White Lion showdown in your campaign prologue.

### Ominous Beginnings
Bizarre alignments, conspicuous occurrences. Strange omens inspire the settlement to celebrate the transformational currents of existence.

Discover portents hidden throughout this expansion (new settlement events, hunt events, and more). Innovate interpreters of fate and celebrate the vicissitudes of life with a Metamorphosis Festival.

### Gambler's Chest Compatible
Arc survivors feasting on Frogdogs will discover specialized cuisine, Knowledge, stomach churning recipes, and more.

Scouts hunting the most challenging Frogdogs discover new techniques for facing their leaping foe.

## Combat Mechanics

### Bouncing
Following the monster, the survivors launch into the air on the spring fauna. Monster and survivor will leverage the bouncing terrain for devastating aerial maneuvers.

Mammoth Leaves monster terrain make your showdown take flight.

### Farting
The bloated monster's complex sphincter is the heinous origin of the survivors suffering. Releasing charged bubbles of mind-bending stench that pursue the survivors as they attempt to evade the monster's gassy attacks.

Fart deck represents the monster's offensive offense.

## Frogskin Creations - Abundant Monster

The massive monster's meaty remains are used to create a host of new tools for beginning settlements. Armor that enables new tactical maneuvers, weapons imbued with the power of insanity, and strange decoction of organs to aid their struggle.

Froskrafter bristling with useful gear for the early era of your settlement:
- Armor sets and corresponding miniatures
- A host of new weapons and tools
- Pattern cards and gear
- Seed Patterns and gear

A monster that inspires:
- Fighting arts
- Secret fighting arts
- Arc survivor knowledges
- Scout Discovery and Tablet

## Bullfrogdog - A Vicious Variant

Survivors may discover the dental-divergent Bullfrogdog. Laden with enormous tusks, the skittish behemoth transforms into a charging horror.

Use the included Bullfrogdog cards to transform the Frogdog into a completely different foe. Fight with precision to harvest the unique resources only a Bullfrogdog has.

Defeating the Bullfrogdog, survivors will craft useful gear beyond their early lantern years including a second set of monster armor.

Includes:
- Bullfrogdog AI and HL cards
- Bullfrogdog resources
- Tuskworks settlement location
- Late campaign armor and gear

## Lore Notes

### Ritika's Story
- Fought against Spidicules (the invisible spider monster)
- Battle took place on "stone faced ground" - the Plain of Faces
- Monster was severely wounded ("remaining legs", "battered body")
- Exposed to "greasy miasma" - toxic emanations from the wounded monster
- The poison burned her eyes and choked her lungs
- "Legions of..." - likely referring to Spiderlings (Spidicules' offspring)

### The Poison Partisan
- Named after fighting through poisonous conditions
- "Partisan" suggests guerrilla-style fighter or pole weapon user
- Crafted using Aggregate Silk Gland resource

### Aggregate Silk Gland
- Spidicules indomitable resource
- "Aggregate" suggests combined/collected silk
- Used in Poison Partisan gear crafting

### Combat Environment
- Stone faced ground - confirms Plain of Faces setting
- Toxic atmosphere from wounded monsters
- Survivors must endure environmental hazards while fighting

### Connection to Spidicules Lore
- Spidicules is invisible, making combat difficult
- Spiderlings swarm as "legions"
- Poison/toxins are key thematic elements

## Lore Details

### The Passage of Time
"Another lantern on the hoard fizzles into darkness. A new lantern year begins."

**Lore Note:** This poetic description suggests:
- Time in Kingdom Death is measured in "lantern years"
- Each year is represented by a lantern that eventually burns out
- A "hoard" of lanterns represents the passage of multiple years
- The fizzling out represents the end of a year/era

### Lunar Twilight Knight
- A variant of the Twilight Knight
- Associated with lunar/moon themes
- Arrives at the beginning of a new year

### Frozen Survivor - Camellia
- A survivor character associated with cold/ice themes
- Named after the flower Camellia

### Wendy, the Frozen Star
- A named character associated with cold/ice and stars
- "Frozen Star" suggests celestial ice themes

### Smog Armor Set
From community spotlight: "The mask effect on the model's face is very unique. It's giving a chilling Venetian mask effect."

**Lore Note:** The Smog armor set features a distinctive mask with Venetian-style aesthetics, suggesting a connection to theatrical or carnival themes.

### Gunborg and Goblin Snarfer
From community spotlight: "Toadpainter perfectly depicts the parasitic relationship of Gunborg and her beloved blade Goblin Snarfer. A truly toxic relationship, the sentient grand weapon feeds off its wedded wielder's basest drives."

**Lore Note:**
- **Goblin Snarfer** is a sentient grand weapon
- It has a parasitic relationship with its wielder Gunborg
- The weapon "feeds off its wedded wielder's basest drives"
- This is described as a "truly toxic relationship"
- The wielder is "wedded" to the blade - suggesting a binding ritual or contract

### Zachary - 15th Anniversary
From community spotlight: "Zachary is a certified hunk. Looking absolutely yoked in his Wrought Lantern Mantle, it's clear Zachary spent 15 years pumping iron in the Wrought Ironworks."

**Lore Note:**
- **Wrought Lantern Mantle** - A piece of equipment/armor
- **Wrought Ironworks** - A location where survivors can train/work
- Suggests 15 years of dedication to physical conditioning
- "Pumping iron" implies metalworking or strength training

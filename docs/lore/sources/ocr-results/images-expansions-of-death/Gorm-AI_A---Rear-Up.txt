**Rear Up**

**Duration**

When this card is drawn face down, the Gorm laboriously lifts its front legs and ends its turn. All survivors adjacent to the monster are knocked down.

While this card is in play, the Gorm has +5 toughness tokens.

When this card is drawn face up, the Gorm crashes down. All adjacent survivors suffer a random severe injury and gain the **Megalophobia** disorder.

Suffer the **Lunacy** brain trauma and **Remember the Story**.

**Story of the Ageless Man**

You remember the story of a young man in his prime. The man was so grateful for his body's ability to protect his loved ones. He decided to honor it by punching the dark sky above a million times. As long as he never finished his vow, he never aged a single day again.

Your body transforms to its physical peak.
Permanently gain +1 accuracy and +1 strength.
Gain the following ability:

**Ageless**: You may hunt if you are retired. When you gain Hunt XP, you may decide not to gain it. Saviors age unnaturally and cannot use Ageless.

*If the settlement has the **Romantic** Conviction Principle, the survivor **inherits** the **Story of the Ageless Man**.*

**Peerless**: When you gain insanity, you may gain an equal amount of survival.

**Story of the Invisible Hair**

You remember the story of a woman with long hair so fine it couldn't even be seen. She claimed she could feel anything her hair touched. On her deathbed, she cut it all off and gifted a single strand to any who visited her. What was left over was scattered, floating in the wind, waiting to be grasped by those who learn this story.

You gain the **Extra Senses** fighting art and the following ability:

**Leyline Walker**: While there is no armor or accessory gear in your gear grid, gain +3 evasion.

*If the settlement has the **Barbaric** Conviction Principle, the survivor **inherits** the **Story of the Invisible Hair**.*

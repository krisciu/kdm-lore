**Olthawartta**
*- living glossary update -*

**Olthawartta:** At the start of your act, you may declare "Olthawartta X" in a loud, booming voice. If you do, ignore the effects of X special rules until the end of your act among the following:

**Cumbersome, Early Iron, Frail, Sentient, Slow, Unwieldy.** X cannot be more than your total pairs of red and blue affinities. When your act ends, gain +X Hurt XP.

When you trigger your 2nd Hurt XP milestone, gain the **Heart of Olthawartta** secret fighting art.

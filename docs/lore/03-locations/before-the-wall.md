# Before the Wall

## Overview

Before the Wall is a sculpture and expansion content piece depicting Aya the Survivor at the beginning of her journey. It is part of an ongoing series of sculptures following Aya's exploits. [before-the-wall.txt]

## Description

Before the Wall represents the start of Aya's journey (confirmed). She is armed with a Black Sword and Beacon Shield [before-the-wall.txt]. Her deadly weapons suggest a powerful quarry waiting for Aya at the end of her hunt [before-the-wall.txt].

## Expansion Content

Before the Wall includes the Vagabond armor set card detailing the armor worn by Aya and the unique bonus it bestows [before-the-wall.txt]. Two new gear cards from the armor set are also included (confirmed) [before-the-wall.txt]:

- Tabard gear card (speculative - not verified in available source excerpt)
- White Dragon Gauntlets gear card (speculative - not verified in available source excerpt)

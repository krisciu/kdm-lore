## Key Technologies

### Lanterns

The lantern is the most important technology in Kingdom Death:
- **Light source** - The only way to see in the darkness
- **Symbol of hope** - Represents humanity's will to survive
- **Crafted from monsters** - Made from organs and fat
- **Mystical properties** - May connect to the Entity

### Armor Sets

Complete armor sets provide bonuses beyond protection:

| Set | Source | Special Ability |
|-----|--------|-----------------|
| Rawhide | White Lion | Basic protection |
| Leather | Mixed | Balanced stats |
| Phoenix | Phoenix | Fire affinity |
| Lantern | Rare | Light generation |

### Innovations

Innovations represent societal advancement:
- **Language** - Foundation of civilization
- **Ammonia** - Preservation of resources
- **Inner Lantern** - Spiritual development
- **Symposium** - Shared knowledge

## Lore Significance

Technology in Kingdom Death reflects themes of:
- **Adaptation** - Using monsters against themselves
- **Progress** - Building civilization in darkness
- **Sacrifice** - Many technologies require survivor sacrifice
- **Mystery** - Some technologies are gifts from unknown sources

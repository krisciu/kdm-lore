## Community Contributions

The community actively contributes to KDM through:

1. **Fan theories** - Lore interpretation and speculation
2. **House rules** - Balance adjustments and variants
3. **Custom content** - Fan-made expansions and quarries
4. **Painting guides** - Miniature painting resources
5. **Digital tools** - Campaign managers and databases

## Industry Impact

Kingdom Death influenced tabletop gaming:

- **Premium pricing** - Established market for luxury games
- **Crowdfunding model** - Showed potential of Kickstarter
- **Mature content** - Proved audience for adult themes
- **Miniature quality** - Raised expectations for detail

# Illuminated Lady 2

Limit 1 per customer
They appear at the settlement's edge. Visions of desire. Radiating benevolence, beckoning with compassion, embodying longing. Their solitary appearance casts doubt on a survivors sanity. Eventually the entire settlement will confess their own unique vision of the creatures. Moved by desire but wary of their purpose, the settlement wonders what purpose the illuminated figures serve.
(Gentle reminder that this miniature comes in the Screaming God Expansion)
Art: Lokman LamSculpture: Team Death
Illuminated Lady 2 is packaged in a hand-stamped kraft box, and includes:
1 x Illuminated Lady 2 photoresin miniature
1 x Hard plastic Kingdom Death 30mm round base and insert
Large Matte Art Print
Character Art Card
First Run Collector's Edition: Signed and numbered authenticity note

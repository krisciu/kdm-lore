# Death High - Necromancer

Welcome to Death High. Dead set on hoopin'.The team always thought it was her necromancer ex that enchanted her with otherworldly talent for the game. The day they realized the skill was her own, they shed tears of pure adoration and all begin to sport a matching ribbon in their hair.On a personal note, this is Poots's new favorite Death High miniature!
Art: Lokman LamSculpture: Team Death
Death High Necromancer is packaged in a hand-stamped kraft box, and includes:
1 x Death High Necromancer photoresin miniature
1 x Hard plastic Kingdom Death 30mm round insert and base
Large Matte Art Print
Character Art Card
First run collector's edition: Signed and numbered authenticity note
Deathgrey edition limited to 250: Deathgrey authenticity note

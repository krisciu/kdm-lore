# Biomech Butcher

In celebration of an energizing Gen Con, we're offering the Biomech Butcher for a limited time to those who couldn't make the show.
It awoke in the hulk of a derelict craft, trapped in the last remaining intact life-support pod. The Biomech Butcher spent maddening eons scavenging the darkness to yoke its atrophied husk to a new form. Countless moments with nothing but festering thoughts of abandonment and vengeance to twist its mind.
Art: Render by Team Death, Concept by Danny Cruz (Based on a Sketch by FCP)Sculpture: Team Death
Biomech Butcher is packaged in a hand-stamped kraft box, and includes:
1 x Biomech Butcher miniature
1 x Hard plastic Kingdom Death 50mm round base and scenic insert
Large Matte Art Print
Character Art Card
First Run Collector's Edition: Signed and numbered authenticity note

# Ambitionism Survivors

Depicting the peak of the Ambitionism philosophy. Ambitionism is a worldview arc survivors can adopt in the Gambler's Chest expansion. Use these miniatures for your arc survivor campaigns or anywhere a pinnacle of aspiration strides among their fellow survivors.
Cradled in the settlement's striving arms, they answer the insistent call of destiny.
Art & Sculpture: Team DeathAmbitionism Survivors is packaged in a hand-stamped kraft box and limited to 500. It includes:
1 x Ambitionism Survivor female photoresin miniature
1 x Ambitionism Survivor male photoresin miniature
2 x Hard Plastic Kingdom Death 30mm round base and photoresin scenic insert
First run collector's edition: Signed and numbered authenticity note
Deathgrey edition limited to 250: Deathgrey authenticity note
Large matte art print
2x Character art card
Limit 2 per customer

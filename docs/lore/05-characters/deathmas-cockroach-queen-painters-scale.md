# Deathmas - Cockroach Queen Painters Scale

She arrived for the party, leading the chirping creature, voices rose in cheer to meet their arrival. Cockroach Queen is presented in painters scale for collectors and hobbyists to enjoy her festive arrival and twitching companion.
Art & Sculpture: Team Death
The Deathmas - Cockroach Queen Painters scale is packaged in a festive orange box that includes:
1 x Deathmas - Cockroach Queen photoresin minature
1 x Hard plastic scenic base insert
1 x Hard plastic Kingdom Death 50mm base
Large matte art print                                          Limit 1 per customer.

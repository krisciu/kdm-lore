# Death High - Twilight Knight (2024)

Limit 2 per customer.
Welcome to Death High! She's not just book smart, she's sword smart.We took this opportunity to freshen up these sculptures. Correcting Anatomy issues, ensuring fabric and hair told a proper story, enhancing details and bringing out more of the characters unique face that Lokman lovingly illustrated. While not a full remaster, each received quite a bit of attention and care. Thank you for granting us this opportunity to continue to improve!
Art: Lokman LamSculpture: Team Death
The enhanced 2024 Death High - Twilight Knight is packaged in a hand-stamped kraft box and includes:
Photoresin Death High Twilight Knight Female
Hard Plastic Kingdom Death 30mm round insert and base
Large Matte Art Print
Character Art Card

# Detective Twilight Knight

A hard-boiled take on the iconic Kingdom Death Pinup Twilight Knight, the Detective Twilight Knight comes equipped with the Twilight Revolver and Detective Cap promo gear cards, equipment perfect for tackling that one last case.
Art: Lokman LamSculpture: Jonah Gilbert
Presented in a litho-wrapped matte white box, the Detective Twilight Knight includes:
Hard plastic Detective Twilight Knight
Kingdom Death 30mm hard plastic base and insert
Matte Art Print
Detective Cap and Twilight Revolver promo gear cards for use in Monster
Promo cards are intended as light-hearted content that is created for fun and should not be taken seriously in the context of Kingdom Death: Monster. Promos are not considered official additions to the rules and players should add them at the discretion of each player group.

# Champion Weaponsmith

Limited supply.
The Cyclops Knight gathers its chosen artisans for tests of crafting skill. The Champion Weaponsmith arrives burning with white-hot determination only matched by the intensity of her forge.
The Champion Weaponsmith is chosen for her vitality and rude spark of creative energy that is reflected in the masterfully crafted, yet simply named, "Axe."
Artist: Lokman LamSculpture: Jonah Gilbert
The Encore edition is enclosed in a hand-stamped kraft box and includes:
1 x Resin Champion Weaponsmith w/ alternate legs and tools.
1 x Hard Plastic Kingdom Death 30mm round base & insert
6" x 4" Matte print of the original concept artwork on heavy card stock
Character art card

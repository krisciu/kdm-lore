# Death High - White Speaker (2024)

Limit 2 per customer.
Welcome to Death High! There’s more to school than just class, something the Death High White Speaker knows all too well.
We took this opportunity to freshen up these sculptures. Correcting Anatomy issues, ensuring fabric and hair told a proper story, enhancing details and bringing out more of the characters unique face that Lokman lovingly illustrated. While not a full remaster, each received quite a bit of attention and care. Thank you for granting us this opportunity to continue to improve!
Art: Lokman LamSculpture: Team Death
The enhanced 2024 Death High White Speaker is packaged in a hand-stamped kraft box and includes:
Photoresin Death High White Speaker
Hard Plastic Kingdom Death 30mm round insert and base
Large Matte Art Print
Character Art Card

# Hollowheart Rene - Painters Scale

Limit 1 per customer
Released at our painters scale, perfect for hobbyists. Hollowheart Rene battles the oblivion instinct, ever hungering for a taste of defeat.
Art: Lokman LamSculpture: Team Death
The Hollowheart Rene - Painter's Scale is packaged in a festive yellow box that includes:
1 x Hollowheart Rene - Painter's Scale miniature
1 x Hard plastic Kingdom Death 50mm base and insert
Large matte art print

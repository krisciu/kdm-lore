# Female Visionary

Limit 1 per customer
Most live drab lives, nurturing fantasies of their world painted in bold colors. Visionaries are those few who come across relics that empower them and demand they take action. Often these individuals lead many to their deaths, building staircases of corpses they climb toward lofty ideals.
Art: Lokman LamSculpture: Jacques-Alexandre Gillois
Enclosed in a hand-stamped kraft box, the Female Visionary Encore includes:
1 x Resin Female Visionary
1 x Hard Plastic Kingdom Death 30mm round base and insert
Large matte print of the original concept artwork on heavy card stock
Character art card

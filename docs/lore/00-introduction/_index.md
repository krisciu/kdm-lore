# Introduction

## Author's Note

This compendium represents an ongoing effort to collect, organize, and present the rich lore of Kingdom Death: Monster. The world created by Adam Poots is deliberately fragmented and mysterious, with lore scattered across rulebooks, story events, flavor text, and artwork.

> **Note:** Much of Kingdom Death's narrative is intentionally obscure. Theories and interpretations are marked as such throughout this document.

## What is Kingdom Death?

Kingdom Death: Monster is a cooperative board game that combines elements of:
- **Survival horror** - Humanity struggling against nightmarish creatures
- **Resource management** - Building settlements from monster parts
- **Tactical combat** - Grid-based encounters with AI-driven monsters
- **Narrative storytelling** - Rich, dark storytelling through event cards

## The Nature of This Compendium

This document attempts to:
1. **Catalog** all known lore from official sources
2. **Connect** disparate pieces of information into coherent narratives
3. **Theorize** where the lore is intentionally vague
4. **Cite** sources for verification

## How to Read This Compendium

- **Confirmed lore** is presented as fact
- **Theories** are clearly marked with speculation warnings
- **Connections** between topics are cross-referenced
- **Sources** are cited at the end of each major section

## Overview

The Butcher is one of the first nemesis monsters a settlement will face, arriving to challenge burgeoning settlements [official-site/shop/batch-scraped/pariah-expansion.txt]. Officially described as a "Frenzied Berserker" [official-site/shop/core-game/kingdom-death-monster-1-6.txt], the Butcher is classified as a Node 1 Nemesis [NN1] in the campaign structure [official-site/shop/gorm-expansion-1-6.txt]. Unlike quarry monsters that are hunted, the Butcher hunts the settlement, forcing survivors into a desperate defensive battle (confirmed).

# The Butcher

> *"He comes with the lanterns. He leaves with the flesh."*

**Category:** Monster (Nemesis)  
**Type:** Humanoid Horror  
**Threat Level:** High  
**Expansion:** Core Game

---

## Overview

The Butcher is one of the first nemesis monsters a settlement will face, arriving on the timeline to harvest survivors. Unlike quarry monsters that are hunted, the Butcher hunts the settlement, forcing survivors into a desperate defensive battle.

## Physical Description

- **Appearance:** Hulking humanoid figure wrapped in a leather apron
- **Size:** Larger than a human, imposingly built
- **Equipment:** Massive twin cleavers, hooks, and butchering implements
- **Notable Features:** Face obscured, relentless demeanor, bloodstained attire

## The Horror of the Butcher

### Not Quite Human
The Butcher's humanoid form raises disturbing questions:
- Was it once human?
- Is it a transformed survivor?
- Or something wearing human skin as a disguise?

### Purpose
The Butcher appears to:
- Harvest survivors for an unknown purpose
- Collect specific body parts
- Return periodically if not destroyed

## Lore Significance

### Timeline Arrival
The Butcher arrives at specific points in a settlement's development:
- **First Appearance:** Lantern Year 4 (typically)
- **Returns:** If not defeated, or at later intervals
- **Pattern:** Suggests it is sent or summoned rather than wandering

### Theories About the Butcher

#### Servant of Greater Powers
> The Butcher may serve the Entity or another cosmic force, harvesting humanity as a resource.

#### Failed Survivor
> Some theorize the Butcher was once a survivor who was transformed through unknown means.

#### Harvest Agent
> The Butcher's methodical approach suggests it is collecting materials for crafting—but what is being crafted?

## Combat Information

### AI Behavior
The Butcher is characterized by:
- **Relentless Aggression** - Constant pressure on survivors
- **Dual Cleaver Attacks** - Devastating damage potential
- **Hooks and Grabs** - Control tactics
- **Intimidation** - Fear-inducing presence

### Threat Assessment
- **Damage Output:** Extremely high
- **Durability:** Significant
- **Speed:** Moderate but unrelenting
- **Special:** Can hack off limbs permanently

### Resources Gained

| Resource | Rarity | Uses |
|----------|--------|------|
| Butcher's Cleaver | Unique | Weapon |
| Leather Apron | Unique | Armor piece |
| Bloody Organ | Rare | Settlement use |

## The Butcher's Legacy

### Lantern Hoard
Defeating the Butcher grants:
- Significant resources
- Knowledge/insight
- Temporary reprieve

### Ancient Butcher
A more powerful variant mentioned in **Campaigns of Death**:
- Enhanced abilities
- Different tactics
- Greater lore significance

## Story Events

- **Showdown: The Butcher** - Mandatory encounter
- **The Butcher's Dance** - Special event variant
- **Aftermath** - Settlement deals with the trauma

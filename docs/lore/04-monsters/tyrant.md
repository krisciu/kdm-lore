# Tyrant

> *"They rocketed into the darkness above, Scarlet gripping the reticulated horns of the monster as the rushing air threatened to tear her off."*

**Category:** Monster
**Type:** Ancient Being
**Expansion:** Dragon King Expansion

---

## Overview

The Tyrant stands as one of the most enigmatic entities in the Kingdom Death universe - an ancient being of immense power and mysterious purpose. Unlike the mindless predators that typically stalk the darkness, the Tyrant possesses both intelligence and ambition, actively seeking to reshape survivors according to its own grand design. This creature serves as the central antagonist of the People of the Stars campaign, where it raises settlements not for their destruction, but for their transformation.

The Tyrant represents a unique threat in the world of Kingdom Death: Monster, as it does not simply hunt survivors for sustenance or territory. Instead, it molds them in the image of its lost civilization, suggesting a deep connection to some forgotten stellar empire that once existed in the darkness above.

## Physical Description and Nature

The Tyrant appears to be a massive, horned creature capable of flight, with reticulated horns that survivors can grip during aerial combat. Its form suggests both draconic and otherworldly characteristics, befitting its role as a being connected to the stars and ancient civilizations. The creature possesses enough physical power to rocket into the darkness above while carrying passengers, demonstrating its formidable size and strength.

As an ancient being, the Tyrant likely predates many of the other monsters that inhabit the Kingdom Death world. Its intelligence and purpose-driven behavior set it apart from the more primal entities that typically threaten settlements, suggesting it may be one of the oldest surviving remnants of whatever cosmic catastrophe created the current dark reality.

## The People of the Stars Campaign

The Tyrant's most significant role lies in its function as the driving force behind the People of the Stars campaign. Rather than simply threatening a settlement's survival, the Tyrant actively raises and nurtures communities, guiding them through unique stories, advantages, and challenges over the course of their development. This campaign represents a wildly different experience from typical Kingdom Death: Monster gameplay, as survivors must navigate the complex relationship with their otherworldly patron.

The campaign culminates in a final judgment, where the fate of the settlement - whether they ascend to the stars or fall short of the Tyrant's expectations - is determined. This suggests the Tyrant operates according to specific criteria or standards, evaluating whether the survivors have successfully been molded into the image of its lost civilization.

## Lore Significance

The Tyrant represents a fascinating departure from Kingdom Death's typical monster ecology. While most creatures in the setting are driven by basic predatory instincts or alien hunger, the Tyrant demonstrates complex motivations tied to memory, loss, and the desire to recreate something that once was. Its connection to a "lost civilization" implies that the current dark world of Kingdom Death may be built upon the ruins of something far more advanced - possibly a stellar empire that once reached beyond the planet itself.

The existence of someone known as the "Tyrant Slayer" suggests that this ancient being can indeed be defeated, though the circumstances of such a victory appear to involve dramatic aerial combat and significant risk to the victor.

## Overview

Atnas the Child-Eater is a named monster included in the Gambler's Chest expansion for Kingdom Death: Monster. Listed among the expansion's huntable quarries alongside entities such as The Gambler, Godhand, Crimson Crocodile, and Smog Singers, Atnas represents one of the nightmarish predators that settlements must face in their desperate struggle for survival.

The creature's epithet—"the Child-Eater"—marks it as one of the more explicitly horrific entities in Kingdom Death's already grim bestiary. In a world where human settlements cling to existence against impossible odds, where every newborn represents precious hope for the future, a monster that specifically preys upon children embodies a particularly cruel form of horror.

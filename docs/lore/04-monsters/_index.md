# Monsters and Entities

The creatures that inhabit the Kingdom Death universe range from animalistic quarry to godlike entities. Understanding these beings is crucial to survival—and to understanding the deeper lore.

## Monster Categories

### Quarry Monsters
Creatures that settlements can hunt for resources.

| Monster | Type | Difficulty |
|---------|------|------------|
| [White Lion](./white-lion.md) | Beast | Starter |
| [Gorm](./gorm.md) | Amphibian | Early |
| [Sunstalker](./sunstalker.md) | Dragon | Mid |
| [Dragon King](./dragon-king.md) | Dragon | Late |
| [Dung Beetle Knight](./dung-beetle-knight.md) | Insectoid | Mid |
| [Spidicules](./spidicules.md) | Arachnid | Mid |
| [Flower Knight](./flower-knight.md) | Plant | Mid |

### Nemesis Monsters
Creatures that hunt the settlement.

| Monster | Trigger | Threat Level |
|---------|---------|--------------|
| [The Butcher](./butcher.md) | Timeline | High |
| [The King's Man](./kings-man.md) | Armored Strangers | High |
| [The Hand](./hand.md) | Timeline | Extreme |
| [Manhunter](./manhunter.md) | Timeline | High |
| [Slenderman](./slenderman.md) | Dark events | Horror |

### Unique Entities

- [The King](./the-king.md) - Ruler of an unknown domain
- [The Nightmare Ram](./nightmare-ram.md) - Dream horror
- [The Wet Nurse](./wet-nurse.md) - Spawner of horrors
- [Gold Smoke Knight](./gold-smoke-knight.md) - Ultimate challenge

## Lore Connections

Many monsters have deep connections to the setting's lore:
- **The White Lion** - May be connected to the Gold Smoke Knight
- **The Butcher** - Servant of unknown masters
- **The King** - Rules his own domain, possibly from another reality

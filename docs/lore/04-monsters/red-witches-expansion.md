# Red Witches

The Red Witches are a squad of White Speakers acting in tandem as a single nemesis monster for Kingdom Death: Monster. This distinct monster is flanked by a myriad of bewitching components.A trio of enforcers, the Red Witches arrive without mercy in pursuit of the White Speaker cult's most notorious runaways.
Red Witches in your Monster Campaign
Red Witches [NN2] are a node 2 nemesis monster, they test the mettle of settlements further along in their campaign. This expansion replaces the King's Man in Kingdom Death: Monster campaigns.
Escalating Foes
The Red Witches are dispatched by the White Speaker cult to hunt and destroy their enemies. They roam the plain of stone faces searching for signs of their greatest foe, mercilessly upheaving settlements that cross their paths.
Red Witches arrive in waves accompanied by cloaked initiates. Fronted by bellicose Braal. Followed by cruel Nico. Finally, otherworldly Seer arrives for a climactic three monster showdown.
Each Red Witch has a distinct fighting style and unique expression of the cult's blood magic.

# Vignette of Death: White Gigalion

At the depths of despair, the survivors of Deadrock rush into the unknown wastes to discover a lurking in the dark.
Vignettes of Death are ready-to-play stories set in the world of Kingdom Death: Monster.  Vignettes of Death: White Gigalion tells the story of four unique survivors facing off with an aberrant form of a familiar foe. Use this Vignette in conjunction with your core game to battle the White Gigalion in a one-time showdown.
If the survivors of Deadrock are victorious, their story ripples through the world, unleashing the White Gigalion as a quarry monster for all Monster campaigns that include the White Lion monster.
In campaign play, the White Gigalion's menace inspires the settlement's ingenuity. The Catarium is expanded using the Giga Catarium, which includes new gear to build with resources gathered from the White Gigalion.
Vignettes of Death are ideal alternatives to campaign play, easy to jump into without unpacking your beloved settlements.
The Vignette of Death: White Gigalion box includes:
1 x White Gigalion with scenic base insert
1 x Hard plastic 100mm base
1 x 8 page Vignette of Death: White Gigalion small rulebook
4 x Unique vignette survivors (Gadrock, Hungry Basalt, Rock Knight, Breccia)
4 x Vignette survivor cards to create record sheets
4 x Vignette survivor gear grid overlays
2 x New White Gigalion AI cards
1 x White Gigalion Basic Action / Data card
1 x White Gigalion Hit Location
2 x Hooked Claw Strange Resources cards
1 x Strain Milestone card
5 x New Giga Catarium gear cards
1 x Lovelorn Rock gear card
1 x Giga Catarium settlement location extension

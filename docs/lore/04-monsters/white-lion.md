# The White Lion

> *"In the darkness, there is no greater teacher than the White Lion. Those who survive its lessons become hunters. Those who don't become bones."*

**Category:** Monster (Quarry)  
**Type:** Beast  
**Difficulty:** Starter  
**Expansion:** Core Game

---

## Overview

The White Lion is typically the first monster encountered by a new settlement. It serves as both the introduction to hunting and a grim reminder of humanity's place in the food chain. Despite being considered a "beginner" monster, the White Lion is a deadly predator that has ended countless survivor stories.

## Physical Description

- **Appearance:** A massive, mane-covered predator with powerful musculature
- **Size:** Significantly larger than any Earth lion
- **Coloration:** Pale white fur, often stained with the blood of its prey
- **Notable Features:** Oversized claws, powerful jaws, piercing eyes that seem to glow in the darkness

## Behavior and Ecology

### Hunting Patterns
The White Lion is an apex predator that roams the Plain of Faces. It hunts through:
- **Stalking** - Patient pursuit of prey
- **Ambush** - Using the darkness to its advantage
- **Overwhelming force** - Relying on raw power to bring down prey

### Territorial Nature
White Lions are territorial creatures that:
- Mark their hunting grounds
- Return to the same areas repeatedly
- May be drawn to the lights of settlements

## Lore Significance

### Connection to Survivors
The White Lion holds a special place in survivor mythology:
1. **First Hunt** - The founding hunt of most settlements involves a White Lion
2. **Rite of Passage** - Hunting White Lions marks the transition from prey to predator
3. **Resource Provider** - White Lion parts form the basis of early survival gear

### Possible Connection to Gold Smoke Knight
> ⚠️ **Speculation:** Some theorists suggest a connection between White Lions and the legendary Gold Smoke Knight, based on visual similarities and the significance of both in survivor stories.

## Combat Information

### AI Behavior
The White Lion's AI emphasizes:
- Claw attacks with grab effects
- Ground pound abilities
- Intimidation tactics

### Weak Points
- **Blind Spot** - Less effective against attacks from behind
- **Mane** - Can be targeted for critical damage

### Resources Gained

| Resource | Rarity | Uses |
|----------|--------|------|
| White Fur | Common | Armor crafting |
| Great Cat Bone | Common | Weapon components |
| Lion Claw | Uncommon | Katar crafting |
| Shimmering Mane | Rare | Advanced armor |
| Golden Whiskers | Rare | Special items |

## Crafting

### Rawhide Armor Set
The most basic armor set, crafted from White Lion resources:
- Rawhide Headband
- Rawhide Vest  
- Rawhide Gloves
- Rawhide Pants
- Rawhide Boots

**Set Bonus:** Basic protection for new survivors

### Notable Weapons
- **Bone Blade** - Basic sword
- **Cat Gut Bow** - Ranged option
- **Lion Katar** - Specialized claw weapon

## Story Events

The White Lion features in several narrative moments:
1. **Prologue** - The First Story begins with survivors awakening near a White Lion
2. **Returning Lion** - The lion may return to hunt the hunters
3. **Pride of Lions** - Settlement may face multiple lions

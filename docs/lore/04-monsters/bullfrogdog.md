# Bullfrogdog

> *"At the farthest edges of the darkness, where even the Frogdog's croaking song grows faint, something larger answers. Tusked. Waiting."*

**Category:** Monster
**Type:** Quarry (Variant)
**Expansion:** Frogdog Expansion

---

## Overview

The Bullfrogdog is a fearsome variant of the Frogdog that emerges only when a settlement's hunters have grown strong enough to threaten even the mightiest of its lesser kin. Lurking at the farthest edges of the darkness—those uncharted voids where light has never reached—this tusked horror represents an escalation of the threat that began with the first plaintive croaks heard beyond the lantern halo.

Where the common Frogdog is described as cagey and revolting, the Bullfrogdog is something altogether more terrible. Survivors who have grown comfortable hunting the stench-belching behemoths they know will find this dental-divergent nightmare a wholly different beast. Its massive tusks transform what was once a skittish, leaping quarry into a charging horror capable of impaling the unwary.

For settlements that have exhausted what the Frogdog can offer, the Bullfrogdog presents both peril and opportunity—a source of unique resources that cannot be harvested from any other creature in the darkness.

## Physical Description

The Bullfrogdog shares the incomprehensible bulk of its lesser variant but is distinguished by its grotesque dental mutations. Enormous tusks protrude from its maw, weapons of terrible power that the creature employs with devastating effect when threatened. The sources describe it as "powerfully toothy," suggesting these tusks may be accompanied by other dental aberrations that make the creature's bite far more lethal than the standard Frogdog.

Despite its fearsome armament, the Bullfrogdog initially exhibits skittish behavior—a deceptive timidity that belies the charging horror it becomes once provoked. Survivors must approach with precision, understanding that the beast's transformation from prey to predator can occur in an instant.

*[Speculative: The "dental-divergent" nature of the Bullfrogdog may suggest some form of mutation or adaptation occurring in Frogdogs that survive long enough in the deeper darkness, their bodies warping to meet unknown pressures.]*

## Behavioral Patterns

The Bullfrogdog's AI represents a completely different showdown experience from its common counterpart. While both creatures share the same basic physiology—the stench bubbles, the leaping attacks, the pursuit of bioelectric signatures—the Bullfrogdog's tusked arsenal fundamentally changes how it fights.

The creature's skittish nature may serve as a survival mechanism, allowing it to assess threats before committing to its devastating charges. Hunters who mistake this initial hesitation for weakness rarely survive to report their error.

## Lore Significance

The emergence of the Bullfrogdog as settlements progress represents one of Kingdom Death's core themes: the darkness adapts. As survivors grow stronger, so too do the horrors that hunt them. The Bullfrogdog is not merely a larger Frogdog—it is an answer to human persistence, a new threat called forth by the very success of those who would carve survival from the endless night.

The unique resources harvested from the Bullfrogdog and the Tuskworks location that processes them suggest that these creatures possess materials fundamentally different from their lesser kin—substances suited for the crafting of late-campaign gear and a second set of monster armor. For settlements that began their journey hunting Frogdogs, the Bullfrogdog represents both the culmination of that path and proof that the darkness always has deeper terrors waiting.

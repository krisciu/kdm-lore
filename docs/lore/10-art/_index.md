### Artistic Influences

Adam Poots draws from various sources:

| Influence | Element in KDM |
|-----------|----------------|
| **Berserk (manga)** | Dark fantasy, body horror |
| **H.R. Giger** | Biomechanical designs |
| **Zdzisław Beksiński** | Nightmarish landscapes |
| **Dark Souls** | Oppressive atmosphere |
| **Classical sculpture** | Miniature proportions |
| **Fashion** | Armor and clothing design |

## Miniature Design Philosophy

Kingdom Death miniatures are characterized by:

1. **High Detail** - Intricate sculpting at small scales
2. **Dynamic Poses** - Action and emotion captured
3. **Modular Assembly** - Customization options
4. **Narrative Elements** - Story told through pose and detail
5. **Mature Themes** - Unflinching horror imagery

## The Role of Art in Lore

Art in Kingdom Death serves lore purposes:
- **Event cards** tell stories through images
- **Monster art** reveals biology and behavior
- **Settlement sheet** shows world-building
- **Miniatures** are canonical representations

# Flower Knight Bust

Limit 1 per a customer
The exquisite Flower Knight, its magnificent countenance captured in a beautiful bust that highlights the intricacies of the Flower Knight's design. Magnifying the dynamic details of the original sculpt and adapting it for the format. Perfect for painting or collecting.
Sculpture: Team Death
The Flower Knight Bust is packaged in a custom box customized with a signed Flower Knight art print and packed in custom cut foam:
1 x Flower Knight bust
1x Oversized signed and numbered collector's card

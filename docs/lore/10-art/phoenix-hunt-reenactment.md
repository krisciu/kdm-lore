# Phoenix  Hunt Reenactment

Limit 1 per customer
The settlement celebrates the unlikely victory over the timeless Phoenix. A whirling dance of iridescent feathers sparkles at the settlement's center enshrining the memory of the settlement's perseverance.
Art: Lokman LamSculpture: Somen Chatterjee + Team Death
Hunt Reenactment - Phoenix is packaged in a hand-stamped kraft box, and includes:
1 x Hunt Reenactment - Phoenix miniature
1 x Hard plastic Kingdom Death 30mm round base and insert
1 x Beta Settlement Event Card
Large Matte Art Print
Character Art Card
First Run Collector's Edition: Signed and numbered authenticity note

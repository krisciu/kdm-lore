# White Speakers

The White Speakers are a blood cult whose savagery once threatened the few enclaves of human civilization. They practice blood magic passed through intricate tales and frenzied rituals.

---

## Origins: The Prince of Swords

There was a settlement of humans so powerful that they defied one of the great entities. In response, the entity drank their only lake and waited for them to turn to dust. Undaunted, the humans grew plants rich in water and survived. 

The furious entity took up its mantle and invaded, wiping out the humans. It left one man as a warning, cursed with an unslakable thirst.

Desperate to end his suffering, the man took up the sword, mastering it with single-minded focus. Exactly 1000 years to the day, the Prince of the Sword stood triumphant over the dying entity. Drinking its blood quenched his thirst for the first time but did not end his torment. Eternally he wanders drinking the blood of worthy adversaries to momentarily satisfy his thirst.

---

## The Cult

White Speakers that tell the story of the Prince of Swords seek the taste of blood stains and remnants of gore on ancient weapons. In this way they gain the prowess of their former wielders.

Long ago, the White Speaker cult's savagery threatened the few enclaves of human civilization. The Order of Twilight Knights rose up nearly destroying the cult. Now, small covens of White Speakers can be found meeting in abandoned places, sharing the secrets of their blood magic, passed on from one White Speaker to another through intricate tales, whispered and screamed in frenzied, transcendental rituals.

---

## White Speaker Nico

Nico is a second White Speaker in the Kingdom Death range. She represents more aspects of the White Speaker cult, their ritual garb and weapons.

---

## Notable Facts

- **Blood Magic:** White Speakers practice blood magic learned through ritualistic storytelling
- **Enemy of Twilight Order:** Nearly destroyed by the Twilight Knights
- **No Male Members:** There are no male White Speakers (see: Mountain Man lore)
- **Secret Covens:** Now operate in small, hidden groups

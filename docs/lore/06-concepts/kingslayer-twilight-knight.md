# Kingslayer Twilight Knight - Painters Scale

Limit 1 per customer
Released at our painters scale, perfect for hobbyists.
Settlements may grow strong leaders that rise above other survivors, exposing them to the scrutiny of the order's enigmatic agenda. Hooded knights stride in from the darkness bringing ruin to these figureheads and departing with their actual heads clutched in bloody hands.
Art & Sculpture: Team Death
The Kingslayer Twilight Knight is packaged in a festive yellow box that includes:
1 x Kingslayer Twilight Knight minature
1 x Hard plastic base insert
1 x Hard plastic Kingdom Death 50mm base
Large matte art print                                          Limit 1 per customer.

# Grimmory

Deathpink Edition only. VERY LIMITED
*BLACK FRIDAY LIMITED AMOUNT OF DEATH PINK GRIMMORY ONLY*
Guilt is the force that drives Grimmory. Betrayals mar his conscience, sins for which he can never fully atone. His shoulders are heavy with death and his heart is lost in the past. His Knives serve two masters. Grimmory believes he is the stronger of them.
The First Run Collector's Edition is enclosed in a hand-stamped kraft box, limited to 500 and includes:
Photoresin Grimmory
1 x Hard Plastic Kingdom Death 30mm round insert and base
Large Matte Art Print
Character Art Card
Beta Rules - 1 x Glossary Card, 2 x Gear Cards
First run collector's edition: Signed and numbered authenticity note
Deathgrey edition limited to 150: Deathgrey authenticity note
DeathPink Edition

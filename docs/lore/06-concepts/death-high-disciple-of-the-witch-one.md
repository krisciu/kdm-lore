# Death High - Disciple of the Witch One

LIMIT ONE PER CUSTOMER
Welcome to Death High. School isn't all toil and trouble.
Art: Lokman LamSculpture: Team Death

The Death High Disciple of the Witch is packaged in a hand-stamped kraft box and includes:
1 x Photoresin Death High Disciple of the Witch
1 x Hard Plastic Kingdom Death 30mm round insert and base
Large Matte Art Print
Character Art Card
First run collector's edition: Signed and numbered authenticity note
Deathgrey edition: Numbered Deathgrey authenticity note

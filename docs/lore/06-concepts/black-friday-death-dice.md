# Black Friday Death Dice

Exclusive to Black Friday
A set of 9 custom Kingdom Death: Monster dice. KDM seasonal dice offer brand-new palettes for your Monster campaign. The  blackest Black Friday Dice are twice tumbled to create a unique matte finish and pleasing tactile sensation. Plunge into darkness and roll to your victory... or your demise!
5 lantern d10s - ten-sided dice with custom KDM numbers and a lantern on the ten face.
5 blackest dice with ash symbols
4 hit location dice - six-sided dice with custom KDM symbols representing the locations of the body (head, body, body, waist, legs, arms).
4 blackest dice with ash symbols

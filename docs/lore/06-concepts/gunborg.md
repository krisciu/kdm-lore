# Gunborg

Gunborg remembers too much. Lifetimes of faithlessness crowd out her sense of self, leaving a being driven by recurring impressions, an instinct to betray the ones who hold her dear. A parasitic weapon greedily feeds off her basest drives, preserving her vacant existence.
Art: CherrylichSculpture: Team Death
Gunborg is packaged in a hand-stamped kraft box, and includes:
1 x Gunborg photoresin miniature
1 x Hard plastic Kingdom Death 30mm round insert and base
Large Matte Art Print
Character Art Card

# Black and Red Dice

A set of 9 custom Kingdom Death: Monster dice, presented in the Red Witches colorway for your next Monster campaign.
5 lantern d10s - ten-sided dice with custom KDM numbers and a lantern on the ten face.
4 red dice with black symbols
1 (inverted) black die with red symbols
4 hit location dice - six-sided dice with custom KDM symbols representing the locations of the body (head, body, body, waist, legs, arms).
3 red dice with black symbols

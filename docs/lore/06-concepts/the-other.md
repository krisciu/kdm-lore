# The Other / The Entity

> *"It does not speak. It does not demand. It simply is—and in its presence, we become something less. Or perhaps, something more."*

**Category:** Concept / Cosmic Entity  
**Also Known As:** The Entity, The Other  
**Source:** Core Rulebook, Various Expansions

---

## Overview

The Entity (often referred to as "The Other") is one of the most mysterious and significant forces in the Kingdom Death universe. It appears to be a cosmic power responsible for—or at least deeply connected to—the creation of the world, monsters, and possibly humanity itself.

## Nature

### What Is The Entity?

The Entity defies easy categorization:
- **Not a monster** - Cannot be fought in the traditional sense
- **Not a god** - Doesn't demand worship (though some worship it)
- **Not sentient** - Or at least, not in a way humans understand
- **Creative force** - Responsible for bringing things into existence

### Manifestations

The Entity manifests through:
1. **Gifts** - Special abilities granted to survivors
2. **Creations** - Monsters that serve its purpose
3. **Influence** - Subtle effects on reality
4. **Presence** - A feeling of being watched/shaped

## Relationship with Humanity

### Creator or Captor?

Evidence suggests the Entity may have:
- Created humans in this world
- Brought humans from elsewhere
- Shaped humans from existing material
- Trapped humans for unknown purposes

### The Entity's Gifts

Some survivors receive "gifts" from the Entity:
- **Saviors** - Individuals with supernatural abilities
- **White Speakers** - Those who hear the Entity's "voice"
- **The Chosen** - Marked for special purposes

## Relationship with Monsters

### Monster Origins

The Entity may be responsible for:
- Creating all monsters
- Directing monster behavior
- Harvesting through monsters
- Evolving monsters over time

## Theories

### The Farming Theory
> **Confidence:** Speculative

The Entity may cultivate humanity like crops:
- Settlements as "farms"
- Campaigns as harvest cycles
- Survivors as resources to be collected
- Death as the harvesting process

### The Dream Theory
> **Confidence:** Speculative

Reality may be the Entity's dream:
- The world exists because it imagines it
- Monsters are nightmares given form
- Survivors are fragments of consciousness
- Waking would end everything

### The Evolution Theory
> **Confidence:** Likely

The Entity may be guiding human evolution:
- Pressures through monsters
- Selection through survival
- Enhancement through gifts
- Goal: Create something new

## Worship and Opposition

### Entity's Cult

Some survivors worship the Entity:
- Believe it is a god
- Seek its favor through sacrifice
- May receive corrupted gifts
- Often lead settlements to ruin

See: [The Entity's Cult](../02-factions/entitys-cult.md)

### White Speakers

Mystics who claim to hear the Entity:
- Prophesy future events
- Interpret Entity's will
- May be genuine or deluded
- Dangerous either way

See: [White Speakers](../02-factions/white-speakers.md)

### Twilight Order Opposition

The Order actively opposes Entity influence:
- Hunt Entity-connected creatures
- Protect settlements from corruption
- Seek to understand without submitting
- May know Entity's weaknesses

See: [The Twilight Order](../02-factions/twilight-order.md)

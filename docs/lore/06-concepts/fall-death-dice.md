# Fall Death Dice

Limited to Fall
A set of 9 custom Kingdom Death: Monster dice. KDM seasonal dice offer brand-new palette for your Monster campaign. The Fall Death dice evoke the crunch of dried leaves underfoot and butternut squash.
5 lantern d10s - ten-sided dice with custom KDM numbers and a lantern on the ten face.
4 brown dice with apricot symbols
1 (inverted) apricot die with brown symbols
4 hit location dice - six-sided dice with custom KDM symbols representing the locations of the body (head, body, body, waist, legs, arms).
3 brown dice with apricot symbols
1 (inverted) apricot dice with brown symbols

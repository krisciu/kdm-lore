# Classic Death Dice

A set of 9 custom Kingdom Death: Monster dice. The classic design is the same dice included with the Monster core game.
5 lantern d10s - ten-sided dice with custom KDM numbers and a lantern on the ten face.
4 black dice with white symbols
1 (inverted) white dice with black symbols
4 hit location dice - six-sided dice with custom KDM symbols representing the locations of the body (head, body, body, waist, legs, arms).
3 black dice with white symbols

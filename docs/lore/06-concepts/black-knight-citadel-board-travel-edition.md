# Black Knight Citadel Board - Travel Edition

Limit 1 per customer
Mini-scale scale. A preposterously small rendering of the Black Knight's crumbling citadel. A delightful bird's eye view of the airborne, thrashing action. Bounce among the tiny elastomer statues and ring the baby bell altars.
People may ask why it's so expensive. This one is a labor of love and a ton of resin. Despite the miniscule size, this mini-scale project was immense in both photoresin quantities and the dedicated effort of our team to print, clean, and process every absurdly itty bitty bit.+25 Units added.+25 Units added.
Sculpture: Team Death
Black Knight Citadel Board - Travel Edition s packaged in a hand-stamped kraft box, and includes:
16 x Black Knight mini-scale showdown board tiles
1 x Black Knight mini-scale mini
11 x Elastomer Statues mini-scale minis
10 x Bell Altar mini-scale minis
112 x Noble Urn mini-scale minis
1 x Kale mini-scale mini
1 x Morg minis-cale mini
1 x Rene mini-scale mini
1 x Marchioness mini-scale mini
1 x mini showdown art card

# Golden Bat Death Dice

A set of 9 custom Kingdom Death: Monster dice, presented in the Golden Bat monster colorway for your next Monster campaign.
5 lantern d10s - ten-sided dice with custom KDM numbers and a lantern on the ten face.
4 black dice with gold symbols
1 (inverted) gold die with black symbols
4 hit location dice - six-sided dice with custom KDM symbols representing the locations of the body (head, body, body, waist, legs, arms).
3 black dice with gold symbols

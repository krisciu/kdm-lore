# Deathmas Percival - Painters scale

Limit 1 per customer
Deathmas Percival unwraps a demented gift intended to bring nothing but tears. Much to the laughing benefactors chagrin, a lifetime bond emerges yapping into Percival's world. Enjoy the painters scale scene perfect for hobbyists and collectors.
Art: Lokman LamSculpture: Team Death
The Deathmas Percival - Painters Scale is packaged in a festive yellow box that includes:
1 x Deathmas Percival - Painters Scale miniature
1 x Hard scenic base insert
1 x Hard plastic Kingdom Death 50mm base and insert
Large matte art print
